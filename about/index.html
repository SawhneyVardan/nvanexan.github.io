Nick is a privacy lawyer and software development consultant practicing in Toronto. He advises organizations that are creating new, disruptive products in conditions of uncertainty on matters related to privacy law, litigation, product design and development.

Legal Experience

Nick graduated from the University of Toronto Faculty of Law in 2011. He received several awards there, including prizes for the highest marks in Telecommunications and Internet Law.

Nick was called to the Ontario bar in 2012 and practiced as a litigation associate at a prominent Bay Street law firm for a number of years. During that time, Nick acted for several companies you may have heard of, including Google, General Motors and Rogers Communications, in connection with high stakes litigation.

Nick has appeared before the Ontario Court of Appeal and the Superior Court of Justice and has worked on matters before the Federal Court of Canada, the Ontario Securities Commission and the Patented Medicine Prices Review Board.

Tech Experience

Nick is a skilled software developer and designer. He has spent years working with and leading Agile software development teams, and is one of the leading engineers of Barometer, a regulatory compliance management tool offered by D+H Financial Solutions, a global leader in fintech.

Nick has extensive experience with JavaScript, React, Angular, WebPack, CSS / SCSS Modules and Protractor. Nick uses command line Git in his daily practice. He writes in markdown. He uses kanban boards to organize work. He automates everything he can and passes the savings on to his clients.
